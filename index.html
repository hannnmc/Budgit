Budgit is a mobile/ desktop web application that allows you to simplfy the way you manage your expenses and help you maximize your budget!


  • To start, click the local_atm icon on the left nav-bar to enter your monthly income.

  • Enter daily income/expense by selecting the appropriate category in the drop-down menu.

  • More features coming soon:

    • pie_chart Monthly categories
    • bar_chart Detailed daily dashboard
    • savings Savings calculator
    • tips_and_updates Cost-cutting recommendations
